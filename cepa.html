Over the last few years, there has been a growing call for the professionalisation of the events industry in Southern Africa.

Who is CEPA? The Council for Event Professionals Africa was initially established in 2013 as a three-way partnership between EXSA (The Events & Exhibitions Association of Southern Africa), SAACI (the Southern African Association for the Conference Industry) and IFEA (the International Festival & Events Association). In 2019, it was incorporated as a Council within SACIA, with a specific mandate to develop professional designations for event managers.
